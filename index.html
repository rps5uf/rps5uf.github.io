About

Rob Shimshock is a journalist and satirist in the Washington, D.C. area. He is currently a fellow at The Daily Caller, where he reports on administrative abuse, political correctness, and 18 to 22-year old crybullies at university campuses. He also contributes satirical commentary to The Rebel Media and Campus Reform.

Before starting with The Daily Caller, Shimshock reported on education for Campus Reform and technology for Breitbart News. He was also a Charles Koch Institute and National Journalism Center intern.

Shimshock graduated from the University of Virginia with a Bachelor of Arts in English and Media Studies. While at UVA, he worked as a print and video reporter, designed and scripted computer games, performed advocacy for the student judiciary, and delivered humiliating defeats to rivals of the table tennis C-team.

When he's not reporting or writing satirical detective short stories, Shimshock can be found playing classical and contemporary piano pieces. A video archive of his performances can be found here.
